## Create Your Digital Twin

Creating your digital twin is a straightforward process. You can either use the Hubspot form or directly through the platform.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Form Submission" icon="envelope-open-text">
    ### Hubspot Form

    You were sent a link to the Hubspot form. What now?

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Complete the Form">
        Complete the form to the best of your knowledge and click "Submit"
      </Step>
      <Step title="The Digital Twin Magic Begins">
        Praxis will be notified of your submission and start to build your digital twin.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Get Started">
        Once your digital twin has been brought to life, you will be sent login credentials. You are now free to play\!
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Signup" icon="right-to-bracket">
    ### Directly through the Platform

    Want to build your digital twin directly?

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign into Gepetto- the Digital Twin Creator">
        Click this joining link to request access to Gepetto: https://www.hiimpria.ai/login?join=am9pbklkPTM4MTY4MWQyLTM2NDQtNDU5Ni04OTkwLTM2NTEzZTNjODk5ZSZyZWY9NjU2ZWFiNjg2Yzc2ZTIwMDQ4NmZmOTJl&brandingId=6992a46d-a52a-422e-9f78-d7986db3e42b Once you are approved, you are ready to begin the process.
      </Step>
      <Step title='Launch the "Digital Twin Creation Assistant"'>
        In the main interface, click the <Icon icon="sparkles" />Assistant button located on the text bar. Open the "Digital Twin Creation" assistant. Begin your assessment.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save your Prompt and Picture URLS">
        Once you've generated your desired digital twin prompt, name and pictures, be sure to save them in a separate browser for ease of use.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add a Digital Twin to your Gallery">
        Click your profile settings, and click the <Icon icon="arrow-right-arrow-left" /> Accounts button. At the top of your digital twin gallery, you will see an "Add" button. This will allow you to compile all of the elements you built to create a fully formed digital twin.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Begin!">
        Once you've saved your changes, you are ready to conversing with your digital twin\!
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
