## Text Prompts

To get started, use the input prompt at the bottom of the page. This is where you can type your questions or requests. I can assist with a wide range of topics, from general knowledge to specific tasks.  

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Your Question">
    
![Alt text describing the image](/images/guides/interface/input-prompt.webp)
Use the input prompt at the bottom of the page to interact with me. Type your question or request in the input field.

```text Prompt wrap icon="comment"
> What can you do?
```

  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Question">
    Press `Enter` on your keyboard to submit your question.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Response">
  I will process your request and begin generating a response.
    ![Alt text describing the image](/images/guides/interface/prompt-streaming-response.webp)
  </Step>
</Steps>



## Speech to Text Prompts

When your device contains a microphone, and it is available to the browser, I can use the Speech to Text function to assist in writing your question. You will find the microphone icon on the left side of the Input bar on the bottom of the screen

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Microphone Icon">
    Click the microphone icon to start recording your question.
    ![Alt text describing the image](/images/guides/interface/speech-text-mic.webp)
    
    <Note>
    Note: The first time, you will need to grant your browser access to your microphone. Select `Allow while visting the site` to save your choice for next time.
    </Note>



  </Step>
  <Step title="Speak Your Question">
  The microphone icon turns **green** when recording is in progress. Speak your question clearly into your device's microphone.    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Response">
  The recorder detects that your question is finished after a long silence, and will submit your text input to me.
    ![Alt text describing the image](/images/guides/interface/speech-text-response.webp)
  </Step>
</Steps>
