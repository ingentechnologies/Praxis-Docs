## Input Prompts

To get started, use the input prompt at the bottom of the page. This is where you can type your questions or requests. I can assist with a wide range of topics, from general knowledge to specific tasks.  

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Your Question">
    
![Alt text describing the image](/images/guides/interface/input-prompt.webp)
Use the input prompt at the bottom of the page to interact with me. Type your question or request in the input field.

```
> What can you do?
```

  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Question">
    Press `Enter` on your keyboard to submit your question.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Response">
  I will process your request and begin generatimg a response.
    ![Alt text describing the image](/images/guides/interface/prompt-streaming-response.webp)
  </Step>
</Steps>
