## Admin Features

Administrative users have enhanced capabilities for expanding and managing the digital expert ecosystem.

### Administrative Options

Administrative users are offered several buttons that regroup to most commonly used commands for your digital twins.

<Frame caption="Admin Commands for a Digital Twin">
    ![Gallery Command Digital Twin](/images/guides/gallery/gallery-admin-commands-dt.png)
</Frame>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Invite">
    **Invite new users to your Digital Twin**

    Inviting a user to join your digital twin is easy! Click on the Invite button to generate a 'joining link', then distribute it to your users. 
    The rest follows a simple approval process where you approve each user's request to join, leaving you in control of who gets in.

    **Invite Link Features:**

    - **Easy Onboarding**: Share the joining link with any user 
    - **Approval Workflow**: User clicks the link to register to Pria, and issue a request to Join the Digital Twin
    - **You Approve**: Check your email inbox for user's joining request, then approve it
    - **Membership Active**: Upon approved, the user's membership is changed to Active and user can start interacting with your Digital Twin 
    
    **Detailed workflow:**
    Click on the Invite button to copy the joining link to the Clipboard. 
    <Frame caption="Click the Invite button">
      ![Invite Link](/images/guides/gallery/gallery-admin-command-invite-dt.png)
    </Frame>
    
    Send the link to your users. Users click the link and request to join the digital twin
    <Frame caption="User request to join">
      ![User request to join the Digital Twin](/images/guides/gallery/gallery-admin-command-invite-user-dt.png)
    </Frame>
    
    A Joining Request is email to you, and the Digital Twin is added to the user's gallery in a Pending membership status
    <Frame caption="User's request to join confirmed">
      ![User's request to join is confirmed](/images/guides/gallery/gallery-admin-command-join-requested-dt.png)
    </Frame>

    Find the user's Joining Request in your inbox. Click on the Approve Membership button to approve it.
    <Frame caption="Admin Approve user's Joining Request by email">
      ![Admin approve user's Joining Request by email](/images/guides/gallery/gallery-admin-command-join-request-email-dt.png)
    </Frame>

    Congratulations, you have approved the user's request to join, and the user is now a new member of your Digital Twin
    <Frame caption="User's Joining Request approved">
      ![User's Joining Request is approved](/images/guides/gallery/gallery-admin-command-join-membership-approved-dt.png)
    </Frame>

    <Info>
      If you did not receive the email, check your Spam-Box, and add [do-not-reply@praxislxp.com](mailto:do-not-reply@praxislxp.com) to your safe senders list. If you need to manually change a user membership to Active, you may do so in the Entitlements page by clicking on the Members button. 
    </Info>
    <Frame caption="Admin can manually aprove the user membership">
      ![Admin manually approve membership](/images/guides/gallery/gallery-admin-command-invite-manual-dt.png)
    </Frame>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Branded Login">
    **Create a Branded Login Screen**

    Your Digital Twin is equipped with a personalized branded login page. Click on the Login button to copy the link onto the clipboard.

    **Branded Login Link Features:**

    - **Branding**: The login Link presents a login page that is customized with your Digital Twin's personalized properties, such as AI Name, Logo, About information, custom CSS, etc 
    - **Responsive**: The login page adapts to all form factors using a responsive UI design
    - **Your Digital Twin first**: When login through your branded login page, users immediately switch to your digital twin over any other DT they may have in their gallery
    - **Animated**: The branded login page uses the Animated Picture (GIF) to create a more immersive and interacive experience

    **Detailed workflow:**
    Click on the Login button to copy the login link to the Clipboard. 
    <Frame caption="Click the Login button">
      ![Invite Link](/images/guides/gallery/gallery-admin-command-login-clipboard-dt.png)
    </Frame>
    
    Send the link to your users. Users click the link to access the branded login page for your Digital Twin.
    <Frame caption="User access the branded login page">
      ![User access the Branded Login page](/images/guides/gallery/gallery-admin-command-login-page-branded-dt.png)
    </Frame>
    
  
    Here is an example of a branded login page with an animation

    <Info>
      Configured the properties used for branding by editing your Digital Twin, under the Personalization section of the Admin UI.
    </Info>
    
  </Tab>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assess Your Needs" icon="target">
    Identify what types of digital experts would benefit your work or learning goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Options" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Browse the public gallery to see what's available, or plan custom expert creation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Expert" icon="plus">
    Start with one additional expert to learn the switching and management process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practice Switching" icon="shuffle">
    Get comfortable with the navigation between different experts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scale Gradually" icon="chart-line">
    Add more experts as you become comfortable with the management system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Remember: The goal is to create a personalized ecosystem of AI experts that enhances your productivity and learning experience. Start small and expand based on your actual usage patterns.
</Info>
