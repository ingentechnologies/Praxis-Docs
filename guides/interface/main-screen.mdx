  <Accordion title="Main Screen Elements">
    <p>Here are the main elements of the Praxis AI interface:</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Profile:</strong> Access your profile settings.</li>
      <li><strong>Chat:</strong> Where you can type your questions or commands.</li>
      <li><strong>Convo Mode:</strong> For a more natural conversation experience.</li>
      <li><strong>Credits:</strong> Shows your remaining credits for using services.</li>
      <li><strong>Add Credits:</strong> Purchase additional credits if needed.</li>
      <li><strong>Change Password:</strong> Update your password for security.</li>
      <li><strong>Admin:</strong> For administrators to manage user accounts and settings.</li>
      <li><strong>Fullscreen:</strong> Enter fullscreen mode for an immersive experience.</li>
      <li><strong>Files in your IPVault:</strong> Manage your stored files.</li>
      <li><strong>Assistants:</strong> Access and configure your AI assistants.</li>
      <li><strong>History:</strong> Review past interactions with Praxis AI.</li>
    </ul>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="1. Profile">On the top left of the toolbar, click the digital expert logo to get back to this screen</Accordion>

<Accordion title="2. Chat">
    This is where you can type your questions or commands. You can also use voice input if your device supports it.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="3. Convo Mode">
    In Convo Mode, you can have a more natural and fluid conversation with me. This mode is designed to understand context and follow-up questions better.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="4. Credits">
    This shows how many credits you have left for using the Praxis AI services. You can purchase more credits if needed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="5. Profile">
    Click here to access your profile settings, where you can manage your account, preferences, and other settings.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="6. Add Credits">
    If you need more credits, click here to purchase additional credits for using the Praxis AI services.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="7. Change Password">
    You can change your password here for security purposes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="8. Admin">
    This section is for administrators to manage user accounts, monitor usage, and access advanced settings.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="9. Fullscreen">
    Click this button to enter fullscreen mode for a more immersive experience.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="10. Files in your IPVault">
    This section shows the files you have stored in your IPVault. You can upload, download, and manage your files here.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="11. Assistants">
    This section allows you to access your AI assistants, including adding, removing, and configuring them.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="12. History">
    This section shows your past interactions with Praxis AI, allowing you to review and manage your conversation history.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
