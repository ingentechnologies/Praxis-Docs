In the Admin Dashboard, you can choose which assistants you would like to enable or disable simply by clicking the blue toggle next to the name.

![Assistant Settings](/images/guides/admin/assistants-settings.webp)

This allows you a quick view of which assistants are shared to everyone using your digital twin

You can also choose to disable assistants temporarily or permanently by clicking the blue toggle button on the top right corner. Should you choose to enable them, make sure the grey X is selected.

![Assistant Disable](/images/guides/admin/assistants-disable.webp)

Make sure to click “Update” at the bottom of your screen to save the changes you’ve made.

![Assistant Update](/images/guides/admin/assistants-update.webp)
