As an administrator, you have access to comprehensive user feedback about your digital twin through the dedicated feedback dashboard. This feature provides valuable insights into how users are interacting with and responding to your AI assistant.


![Admin Feedback Interface](/images/guides/admin/admin-feedback.webp)

Every feedback comment submitted by users is automatically collected and displayed here for your review. Our Praxis AI team actively monitors all feedback and ensures prompt responses to user concerns and suggestions.


Your involvement in reviewing this feedback helps us continuously improve the system. We greatly appreciate any additional insights or feedback you can share with our team!
