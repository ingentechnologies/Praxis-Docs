---
title: "Change Password"
description: "Follow these steps to update your account password and maintain secure access to your digital twin."
icon: "user-lock"
---


<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Password Settings">
    Navigate to your profile settings and locate the password change option. Click the **"Change Password"** button to begin the process.

    <img
      src="/images/password-profile-change-button.webp"
      alt="Profile settings page showing the Change Password button location"
      title="Change Password Button in Profile Settings"
      style={{ width:"83%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Email Address">
    You'll be prompted to enter your email address associated with your account. This ensures the password reset request is sent to the correct email address for security verification.

    <img
      src="/images/password-email-page.webp"
      alt="Email input form for password reset verification"
      title="Enter Email Address for Password Reset"
      style={{ width:"83%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Verification Code">
    Check your email for a verification code sent to your inbox. Enter this security code in the provided field to confirm your identity and proceed with the password change.

    <Note>
      The verification code typically expires within 10-15 minutes for security purposes. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or request a new code.
    </Note>
    <img
      src="/images/password-reset-code.webp"
      alt="Verification code input field for password reset confirmation"
      title="Enter Security Verification Code"
      style={{ width:"83%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your New Password">
    Once verified, enter your new password. Choose a strong password that includes a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters for optimal security.

    <Tip>
      **Password Best Practices:**

      - Use at least 8-12 characters
      - Include uppercase and lowercase letters
      - Add numbers and special characters
      - Avoid common words or personal information
    </Tip>
    <img
      src="/images/password-reset.webp"
      alt="New password creation form with confirmation field"
      title="Create New Password Form"
      style={{ width:"83%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  After successfully changing your password, you'll be automatically logged out of all devices for security. You'll need to log back in using your new password.
</Info>

<Warning>
  Keep your new password secure and don't share it with others. If you suspect your account has been compromised, change your password immediately and contact support at [**humans@praxis-ai.com**](mailto:humans@praxis-ai.com).
</Warning>
