As a user, you can add credits from your profile settings. Remember that if you are using Pria from a university profile, your credits will be supplied from your institution.

![Alt text describing the image](https://placeholdit.com/1000x600/fcefff/c0a1e6)

If you would like to add your own credits for your personal use, click the ‘Add Credits’ button under the profile settings. Then scroll down to the bottom of the page.

![Alt text describing the image](https://placeholdit.com/1000x600/fcefff/c0a1e6)

Simply select the amount of credits you would like and pay.
