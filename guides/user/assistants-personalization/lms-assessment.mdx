## Connecting to Your LMS

When configured by your institution, your digital twin can help you search for content on your LMS (Learning Management System such as Canvas, Praxis LXP, etc.).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask Your Question">
    Start by asking your digital twin about your coursework:

    ```text
    What are the assignments for my course?
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Canvas">
    Click the **Connect to Canvas** link to establish the connection with your LMS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Click **Authorize** to grant your digital twin access to your LMS. You'll see a success message confirming the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return and Ask Again">
    Simply refresh the browser tab to return to your digital twin and ask your question again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Building Custom Assistants 

Transform your learning experience by creating specialized AI assistants that understand your unique educational requirements and learning style.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Focus" icon="palette">
    Decide what type of learning support you need:

    - **Subject Tutors** for specific academic areas
    - **Study Coaches** for learning strategies and motivation
    - **Research Assistants** for project and assignment help
    - **Practice Partners** for skill development and review
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Assistant" icon="gear">
    Customize your assistant's:

    - Knowledge base and reference materials
    - Communication style and personality
    - Learning methodologies and approaches
    - Assessment and feedback preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Content" icon="upload">
    Upload and integrate:

    - Course syllabi and reading materials
    - Personal notes and study guides
    - Practice problems and examples
    - Reference documents and resources
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and Refine" icon="flask">
    Interact with your assistant and:

    - Test its understanding of your materials
    - Adjust its responses and teaching style
    - Fine-tune its knowledge and capabilities
    - Optimize for your learning preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Personalizing Your Learning Journey

Your assistants learn from every interaction to provide increasingly personalized support:

- **Learning Style Recognition**: Identifies whether you're a visual, auditory, or kinesthetic learner
- **Pace Adjustment**: Adapts explanations based on your comprehension speed
- **Difficulty Scaling**: Adjusts challenge levels as your skills improve
- **Interest Tracking**: Focuses on topics and examples that engage you most
- **Progress Monitoring**: Tracks your growth and suggests next steps
